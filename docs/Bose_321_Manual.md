# 🎛️ **BOSE 3·2·1 SERIES II – COMPLETE TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION DOSSIER**

## 📦 System Overview

The **Bose 3·2·1 Series II DVD Home Entertainment System** is a **compact 2.1-channel surround-simulation system** produced from **2004–2006**, offering **DVD/CD playback**, **Dolby Digital & DTS decoding**, and **TrueSpace® spatial processing**.
It was marketed alongside the upgraded **3·2·1 GS Series II**, which featured **Gemstone®** speakers with improved dispersion.

* **Manufacturer:** Bose Corporation, Framingham, MA, USA
* **Model Numbers:** 3·2·1 Series II / 3·2·1 GS Series II
* **System Type:** Integrated DVD/CD media center + Acoustimass® subwoofer + dual front speakers
* **Production Era:** 2004–2008
* **Firmware Platform:** Proprietary Cirrus Logic DSP (Dolby/DTS licensed)
