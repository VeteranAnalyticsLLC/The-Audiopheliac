# Between Stations — Album Brief

**Project:** The Audiopheliac debut album
**Status:** In Progress | Started 2026-05-05
**Workflow:** Original lyrics + vocals / Suno backing arrangement / Ableton mastering

## Concept

The space on the dial between where you expected to land and where the music
takes you. Collision format: the gap between musical context and lyrical
content creates a third meaning neither half would have alone. Throughline:
all American music draws from the same well. The collision format proves it.
